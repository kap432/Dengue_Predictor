Understanding Dengue Prevention

Get essential tips on how to prevent dengue and protect yourself and your loved ones.

Prevention of Dengue

Mosquito Prevention

  • The basic method to control the Aedes species is by damaging their habitat. Normally, mosquitoes grow in stored water. Get rid of stored or opened water.
  • Killing mosquitoes by using biological control agents.
  • Applying various mosquito repellents present in the market.
  • Killing mosquitoes by using insecticides. However, they are harmful to human health.

Vaccine

Normally, people get vaccinated against dengue after their first infection. The immune system makes enough antibodies after the initial exposure to the virus. The current vaccine is only suggested for those already infected, but in some cases, adverse effects have been observed.

Treatment of Dengue

  • There is no specific medication for dengue at present.
  • Treat the symptoms with the help of healthcare providers.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Take proper rest.
  • If you observe any serious warning signs, seek medical attention immediately.
What should I do if I suspect I have Dengue?

If you notice mild dengue symptoms, see a doctor immediately for evaluation and tests. If you have traveled to dengue-endemic areas in the last two weeks, inform the doctor during your consultation.
